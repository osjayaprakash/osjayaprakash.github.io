In the ever-evolving field of Information Retrieval (IR), keeping up with the latest research, trends, and networking opportunities is crucial. However, finding a comprehensive list of IR conferences can be a daunting task. I have compiled an overview of some key IR conferences.


1. CHIIR: Conference on Human Information Interaction and Retrieval

1. SIGIR: International ACM SIGIR Conference on Research and Development in Information Retrieval
  * [SIGIR 2024 Website](https://sigir-2024.github.io/)

1. SIGIR-AP: International ACM SIGIR Conference on Information Retrieval in the Asia Pacific

1. ECNLPIR: Eurasian Conference on Natural Language Processing and Information Retrieval

1. International Conference on the Theory of Information Retrieval

1. KDIR: International Conference on Knowledge Discovery and Information Retrieval

1. CLEF: Conference and Labs of the Evaluation Forum

1. ECIR: European Conference on Information Retrieval

1. TREC: The Text Retrieval Conference. [TREC Proceedings](https://trec.nist.gov/proceedings/proceedings.html)

1. CIKM: Conference on Information and Knowledge Management

1. FLIRT: Workshop on Federated Learning for Information Retrieval

1. SISAP: International Conference on Similarity Search and Applications

1. ACM The Web Conference [link](https://thewebconf.org/)

1. ACM WSDM: Web Search and Data Mining Conference 

1. ACM SIGKDD

1. RecSys

1. IEEE International Conference on Big Data

1. ECDL

1. ASONAM
