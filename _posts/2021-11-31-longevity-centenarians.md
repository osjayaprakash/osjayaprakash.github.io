* Centerians are people who live longer than 100 years. 
* The number of centenarians steadily increase over time.
* Japan leads the chart with 72 centerians per 100K population. USA is 24 and India is last in the chart with 2.1.
* Supercentenarians are people who live longer than 110. Only 73 people lived longer than 115, and only one beyond 120 so far.
* FUN FACT: Centerians often receive letters from leader of their country. In USA, you can request a letter from president on your 100th birthday.
* Facts
  * Tennis players on average live longer by 9.7 years. Also, they live healthy life.
* Recommendations for lifespan
  * IF: Intermittent fasting
    * Eat food in 8 hour window for the day 
    * Not eating for 16 hours (Water, black coffee is ok)
  * Low carb and low calorie diet (keto)
    * Dark choclate (0% sugar, 100% cocoa)
  * Strength activites:
    * Weight workouts
    * Grip strength
  * Cardiovascular fitness:
    * HIIT
    * Running
  * Reduce stress
  * Good relationships: 
    * Have people who care about you and interested in your growth.
    * Avoid toxic places and people
  * Good sleep (recovery)
  * Love the work
    * Minimize the regrets (regret minimization framework)
  * Caring for others
