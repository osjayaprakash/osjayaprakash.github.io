Don Miguel Ruiz written Four Agreements book. The goal of the book to provide guidance to attain personal freedom: living with greater happiness, peace, and authenticity by freeing ourselves from self-limited thoughts snd beliefs. This book is written based on Toltec wisdom. 

The four agreeements are: 

* **Be Impeccable with your word**
  *  Speak with integrity, avoid using words to harm others or yourself, and express yourself honestly.
  *  Take responsiblity for your actions, but do not blame or judge yourself.
* **Don't take anything personally**
  * Understand that others' words and actions are reflections of them not you.
  * Don't let others' words and actions affect sense your self-worth.
* **Don't make assumptions**
  * Avoid interpreting others' actions and thoughts without clarity
  * Communicate openly to prevent misunderstandings
  * Have courage to ask questions until you are as clear as you can be. Even then do not assume you know all there is to know about a given situation.
* **Always do your best**
  * Do your best in any situation. (Whether you are sick or healthy, you can always do your best, the results may vary but mindset is the same.)
  * Be kind to yourself when things don't go as planned.
