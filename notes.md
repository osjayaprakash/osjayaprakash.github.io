## notes

1. **take care of yourself (self-care)**
    * Focus on your health, relationships, and financial well-being, in that order. Proactively address health concerns, as many age-related issues are difficult to reverse. Cultivate healthy habits through sufficient sleep, nutritious food, moderate exercise, and regular mindfulness practices.
    * Minimize stress in both your professional and personal life. Nurture a few trusted relationships, recognizing the complexity of human connections. Extend support to others when possible.
    * **Forgive** yourself for past the mistakes and errors. All human beings make mistake, ofcouse, each mistake comes with the cost.
