Jayaprakash Sundararaj



I am a Senior Software Engineer at Google USA, specializing in search ranking and personalization projects. My work focuses on developing and training deep learning models and leveraging large language models (LLMs) for selected initiatives. Additionally, I play an active role in hiring, onboarding, and mentoring engineers, fostering a culture of growth and excellence within the team. Notably, I launched: [1] Fact checking articles in Google News and Google Web Search, [2] On-Device Univeral Search in Pixel System Intelligence team, and [3] Query recommendations in Play Store Search Home.

I have a post-graduation degree in CSE from IIT Bombay, India & ungraduate degree in IT from Anna University, Chennai.

Previously, I have worked in Yahoo! Research, and Alcatel Lucent Labs.

Interests

I enjoy working in applied research problems, and building usable projects leveraging state of the art machine learning models. Lately, I'm focused on pursing interpretability and explainablity aspect of maching learning research.
